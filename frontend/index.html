Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions? We've got answers. Use the search or filter by category to find what you need.

How do I enroll in a course?

Enrollment is handled through the university's main portal. Once you are officially registered for a course, it will automatically appear on your BSNU LMS dashboard at the start of the semester.

Are lectures available on-demand?

Yes, most lectures are recorded and uploaded to the LMS within 24 hours of the live session. This allows you to review the material at your own pace, anytime and anywhere.

Can I access the LMS on my mobile device?

Absolutely! The BSNU LMS is fully responsive and designed to work seamlessly on all devices, including desktops, tablets, and smartphones. You can access all features through your web browser.

What kind of technical support is available?

We offer technical support via email and phone during university business hours. You can find our contact details in the footer of this page. For course-specific questions, please use the course forum or contact your professor directly.

How do I submit assignments?

Assignments can be submitted through the course page. Navigate to the assignments section, select the assignment, and upload your file or enter your response before the due date.

Is there a mobile app for BSNU LMS?

Yes, BSNU LMS has a dedicated mobile app available for both iOS and Android devices. You can download it from the App Store or Google Play Store.

How can I reset my password?

To reset your password, click on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page. Enter your registered email address, and you will receive instructions to reset your password.
